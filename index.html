To My Forever Home, My Mumma

Happy Birthday, Mumma 💖

Aaj tuzha birthday aahe, pan gift mala milalay... because God chose you to be my Mumma.

Mala mahit nahi ki mi itki lucky kashi aahe, pan ek goshta nakkich mahit aahe... if there's one person I can never imagine my life without, it's you.

Everyone always says:

"You are such a Mumma's girl."

And you know what?

Yes, I love to be Mumma's girl.

In fact, that's the biggest compliment anyone can ever give me. Because being your daughter is my favourite identity.

Mumma, mi kiti hi mothi zale tari jevha ghari yete tevha maze dole tulach shodat astat

Tujhya "Kalji ghe" madhye mala complete world disata.

Tujhya "pohachlyavar Phone kar" madhye mala security diste.

People think these are just normal words...

But for me, they are Home.

Ajunhi kahi changla zhala ki saglyat pahila tula sangaych asta. Ani kahi vait zhala ki pan saglyat pahili aathavan tujhich yete.

Because somehow... you always make everything okay.

Tujhya mithit sagle problems khup chote vatayla lagtat. Tujha haath dokyavar thevlas ki asa vatta ki sagla tension nighun gela.

How did God put so much love in one person's heart?

Because no matter how many mistakes I make, how many times I get angry, or how impossible I become...

You never stop loving me.
Never.

Mumma, mala mahit aahe mi kadhi kadhi khup chidte, attitude dakhavte, busy hote...

Pan ratri jhopnyapoorvi mala he pan mahit asta ki udya divas mumma chya hug nech suru honar ahe...

Mala future chi bhiti nahi...

Pan ek goshtichi khup bhiti vatte...

A life where I can't come running to you.

A life where I can't hear you calling my name.

A life where I can't sit beside you after a long day and tell you every little thing.

Honestly... I don't think I know how to live in that world.

Tu fakta mazi Mumma nahis...

Tu maza confidence ahes.

Tu maza peace ahes.

Tu maza safe place ahes.

Tu maza biggest blessing ahes.

Thank you for loving me even when I wasn't easy to love.

Thank you for standing beside me in every success and failure.

Thank you for making me believe that no matter what happens...

"Mazi Mumma aahe na."

That's enough for me.

Happy Birthday, Mumma ❤️

I love you more than you'll ever know.

Mi nehmich tuzhi choti mulgi rahnar.



With all my love ❤️

Madhura
